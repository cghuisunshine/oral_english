# IELTS Speaking Question Bank With Model Answers

Source index: https://ielts.koolearn.com/20250812/866639.html  
Scope: 2025 September-December IELTS Speaking topics listed by New Oriental Online.  
Note: These are original practice answers, not official examiner answers and not copied from the source.

## How To Use This Bank

Practice Part 1 in 20-30 seconds per answer. Practice Part 2 by preparing for 1 minute and speaking for 1-2 minutes. Use the Part 3 follow-up to extend your answer with reasons, comparisons, and examples.

## Part 1 Quick Interview Bank

1. **Study or work**  
   Question: Do you work or are you a student?  
   Answer: I am a student at the moment, and I am focusing mainly on English and academic skills. My schedule is quite full, but I like it because I can see steady progress.
   Keywords: study work; english academic skills; steady progress; focusing mainly; english

2. **Hometown**  
   Question: What is your hometown like?  
   Answer: It is a busy but friendly city with a mix of modern buildings and older neighborhoods. I like it because daily life is convenient and there are many places to eat and relax.
   Keywords: hometown; friendly city; modern buildings; older neighborhoods; daily life

3. **Accommodation**  
   Question: Do you live in a house or an apartment?  
   Answer: I live in an apartment. It is not very large, but it is comfortable, easy to clean, and close to shops and public transport.
   Keywords: accommodation; shops; public transport; easy; clean

4. **The area you live in**  
   Question: What do you like about your neighborhood?  
   Answer: I like the convenience most. There are cafes, grocery stores, and parks nearby, so I can handle most daily tasks without spending much time travelling.
   Keywords: area; grocery stores; parks; daily tasks; parks nearby

5. **Public places**  
   Question: What public places do people often visit in your city?  
   Answer: People often go to parks, shopping centers, libraries, and sports facilities. Parks are especially popular because they are free and suitable for all ages.
   Keywords: public places; parks; parks shopping centers libraries; sports facilities parks; especially popular

6. **Doing something well**  
   Question: Can you describe something you did well recently?  
   Answer: I recently gave a short presentation in English, and it went better than I expected. I prepared clear notes, spoke slowly, and answered questions confidently.
   Keywords: doing something well; short presentation; clear notes; recently; english

7. **Shoes**  
   Question: What kind of shoes do you usually wear?  
   Answer: I usually wear comfortable sneakers because I walk quite a lot every day. I care more about comfort than fashion, although I still prefer a simple, clean style.
   Keywords: shoes; sneakers; wear comfortable sneakers; walk; lot every day

8. **Rules**  
   Question: Are rules important in daily life?  
   Answer: Yes, rules make shared spaces safer and more organized. For example, traffic rules help people move around without confusion or unnecessary risk.
   Keywords: rules; traffic rules; shared spaces safer; organized; example traffic rules

9. **Working with old people**  
   Question: Have you ever worked with older people?  
   Answer: Yes, I have helped older relatives with phones and online services. It taught me to explain things patiently and avoid using too much technical language.
   Keywords: working old; older relatives; online services; technical language; phones

10. **Crowded place**  
    Question: Do you like crowded places?  
    Answer: Not really. I can accept crowds at concerts or festivals, but in daily life I prefer quieter places because they help me think and relax.
    Keywords: crowded; daily life; accept crowds; concerts; festivals

11. **Growing vegetables**  
    Question: Would you like to grow vegetables?  
    Answer: Yes, I would like to try growing herbs or tomatoes on a balcony. It seems relaxing, and it would be satisfying to eat something I grew myself.
    Keywords: growing vegetables; growing herbs; tomatoes; balcony; seems relaxing

12. **Advertising**  
    Question: Are you influenced by advertisements?  
    Answer: Sometimes, especially when an ad shows a product clearly and gives useful information. However, I usually check reviews before buying anything expensive.
    Keywords: advertising; especially; product clearly; check reviews before buying; anything expensive

13. **Chatting**  
    Question: Do you prefer chatting online or face to face?  
    Answer: For quick messages, online chatting is more convenient. But for serious conversations, I prefer face to face because tone and body language make things clearer.
    Keywords: chatting; face face; body language; quick messages online chatting; serious conversations

14. **Taking a break**  
    Question: How do you usually take a break?  
    Answer: I usually take a short walk, drink water, or listen to music. Even ten minutes away from a screen can help me feel more focused.
    Keywords: taking break; short walk; short walk drink water; listen; music even ten minutes

15. **Sharing**  
    Question: Do you like sharing things with others?  
    Answer: Yes, especially food, notes, and useful information. Sharing makes relationships warmer, and it often encourages others to help you as well.
    Keywords: sharing; especially food notes; relationships warmer; encourages others; well

16. **Plants**  
    Question: Do you keep plants at home?  
    Answer: I keep a small indoor plant near my desk. It is easy to care for, and it makes the room feel fresher and less plain.
    Keywords: plants; indoor plant; keep; small indoor plant near; desk

17. **Friends**  
    Question: How often do you meet your friends?  
    Answer: I meet close friends once or twice a month because everyone is busy. We usually eat together or have a long conversation in a cafe.
    Keywords: close; meet close friends once; twice; month; everyone

18. **Museums**  
    Question: Do you enjoy visiting museums?  
    Answer: Yes, if the exhibition is well designed. I like museums that use stories, objects, and interactive displays instead of only long written descriptions.
    Keywords: museums; interactive displays; exhibition; well designed; stories objects

19. **Going out**  
    Question: Do you often go out in the evening?

    Keywords: going out; during; week; rest after studying; working

20. **Flowers**  
    Question: Do people in your country give flowers as gifts?  
    Answer: Yes, flowers are common for birthdays, graduations, and visits to someone who is ill. They are simple but thoughtful gifts.
    Keywords: flowers; thoughtful gifts; common; birthdays graduations; someone

21. **Puzzles**  
    Question: Do you like doing puzzles?  
    Answer: Yes, I like word puzzles and simple logic games. They are a good way to train concentration without feeling like serious study.
    Keywords: puzzles; word puzzles; logic games; simple logic games; good way

22. **Public transport**  
    Question: Do you often use public transport?  
    Answer: Yes, I use buses or trains when the route is convenient. It is cheaper than driving and I can read or listen to podcasts on the way.
    Keywords: public transport; buses; trains; route; cheaper

23. **Names**

    Keywords: geography; parts; especially learning; countries climates; maps

    Keywords: birthday; keep; simple; dinner; photos
